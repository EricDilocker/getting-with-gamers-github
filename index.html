Volunteer Opportunities

Volunteering for our tournaments is quite an amazing experience! Our events bring thousands of spectators each event who all share a passion within the Gaming genre! Working with us gives our volunteers the chance to become friends with a large surplus of people who all have various interests in the gaming world. Whether you enjoy playing casually at home or spectating live events online, the opportunity to meet new people is guaranteed.

With our weekly events , the opportunity to work with us is very frequent. It is not uncommon for past volunteers to continue working our events as they've had the opportunity to make new friends and in some cases even network. With our connections in the gaming industry, the chance to network with developers from your favorite games is always an option!

Be sure to take the opportunity to fill out our volunteer form for our events and become apart of the GwG family today!

Contact Information

Getting with Gamers is a non-profit company that was started as a local event in the small town of Angleton, Texas. GwG recognizes that technology is continously advancing in all fields, including the gaming industry. With the internet becoming the common solution for most events, GwG believes that the connection of meeting strangers that share a passion with one another is just something that needs to continue happening.

With this, GwG provides a fun and safe enviornment for people of all ages to come and enjoy sharing their love for video games! No matter the genre or platform, GwG provides the necessary tools and equipment for gamers to meet with one another. As GwG is a non-profit company, anyone can come and create grand experiences.

GwG events are currently only located within the USA (not including livestreams). Stay posted for upcoming updates on the status of GwG events becoming an international experience.
